Welcome to

Canary Detect

Canary Detect provides underground utility location and leak detection surveys across the Canary Islands

Using the latest cutting edge technology our team have unrivalled experience and expertise in accurate surveys to pinpoint both the location and depth of buried utility pipes and cables.

Our survey services are cost effective and can save our customers thousands through the unnecessary costs and disruptions of repeated excavations to locate services.

Our leak detection surveys are accurate and use a combination of Gas sensors, CCTV and PipeMic. We offer leak detection for fresh water pipes both internal and external and pool systems.
